About us


Finally, there is a new platform for all artists to present their works and find exposures:

Artify was conceived with a goal to level the playing field for emerging artists, students, underground artists, etc. Too many people nowadays appreciate and buy arts purely on the name of the artists, therefore too many artists with talents are overlooked. And we set out to change that. Here in Artify we deliever you all the arts that are created by local artists, no matter they are underground artists, or art student, or just amateur. Just search artify kiwi, and you will see their works, and view their profile as well.
